About me

I am working as the Associate Professor and Associate Head, Department of Computer Science Engineering at Kalasalingam Academy of Research and Education, Tamil Nadu, India. I completed my Bachelor degree as Anna University Rank Holder in Computer Science and Engineering at Velammal College of Engineering and Technology, and Master degree as Gold Medallist in Computer Science and Engineering at Thiagarajar College of Engineering, Madurai. I had qualified in GATE-2016 and UGC-NET as an Assistant Professor. I had completed my Ph.D on Human Activity Recognition leveraging Deep Learning and Fog Computing Frameworks at Kalasalingam Academy of Research and Education. I had received the PBL India Certification from Aalborg University, Denmark. I developed many consultancy and research projects for Esteemed Industries. I had received seed money grants for projects leveraging deep learning and internet of things. I had published more than 60 research papers in Journals, conferences and Book Chapters, 8 patents and has a record of h-index 12 in Scopus. I am guiding over 8 scholars for PhD and PG Programs. I also serves as faculty mentor for Industrial Projects at Multi-core Ware Inc in the Areas of Activity Recognition and Autonomous Navigation. I had received grants from international societies including IEEE Robotics and Automation Society. My areas of research include Algorithms (Randomized, Approximation, Parallel), Machine/ Deep Learning, Data Analytics, Fog Computing, Image & Video Processing. I also serves as a reviewer in various reputed Journals. I am the professional member of IEEE Robotics and Automation Society and also leads the student chapter of IEEE RAS and Open Source Society. I am the recipient of Best Faculty Awards, TLC Award, PBL Practitioner Award, Best Mentor Awards from IUCEE, NPTEL and National Institutions.
